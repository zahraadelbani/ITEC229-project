ABOUT

Hello there ! My name is Zahraa Delbani, I'm from Banjul, Gambia. I'm a student at Eastern Mediterranean University, studying Information Technology. I have a genuine passion for web development and hope to build my career in this exciting field. Painting is my true artistic outlet, where I can let my creativity flow and create unique and captivating artwork.

In my family, I have three siblings: Sonia, who is studying Pharmacy at EMU, Hawraa, who is still in high school, and Israa, who is happily exploring the world in kindergarten. My parents, Bassem and Lina, have been my pillars of support throughout my journey.

I have a diverse background, being both Gambian and Lebanese. Growing up in Gambia has shaped my values and provided me with a deep appreciation for cultural diversity. My birthday falls on June 17th, 2002, marking the beginning of my life's adventures.

I'm excited to continue my education, embrace new opportunities, and learn and grow in the fields of web development and art.
