90 x 60 cm "Elephant abstract" Elephants are majestic creatures that symbolize strength, wisdom, and grace. My inspiration comes from their natural habitat, such as the African savannah or the lush forests of Asia. 2019
60 x 90 cm "Thoughts" I created the elephant abstract painting as a reflection of my emotional state and personal feelings at that time. Abstract art became my chosen form of expression, enabling me to communicate my innermost thoughts and emotions. Through vibrant colors, dynamic brushstrokes, and abstract forms, I aimed to convey the essence of my mental landscape. This artwork served as a powerful means for me to connect with and share my human experiences in a concise and impactful way.
60 x 90 cm "African Abstract" African abstract art inspired me to create my own masterpiece. This painting is my first attempt, reflecting my personal journey and passion for artistic expression. It's a testament to my growth as an artist and the beginning of a creative exploration that continues to shape me. 2021
60 x 90 cm "The Peacock" A peacock sighting at a hotel sparked my artistic inspiration. I felt compelled to capture its beauty in this painting, preserving the essence of that awe-inspiring moment on canvas.
